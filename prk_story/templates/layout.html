*Method

Public parking includes public on-street and off-street counts from SF OpenData. This count does not include code-required private parking for commerical or residential uses.

Parking stall size is modeled as 9’x18’ per ADA minimums. Parking area model does not include car access lanes or additional accessory parking areas. San Francisco County area referenced from US Census Bureau.

Housing model references 2013 Paragon Real Estate analysis and does not include typical residential accessory areas. Not all of the parking areas may be suitable for residential development.
